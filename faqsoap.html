FAQs about Soaps and Candles

  • How long does it take to make these products?

    Depends on the number of orders at hand, the availability of the raw materials (the desired quantity of wax and clear soap base, organic colours, scents etc.) and the amount of minute details in the desired product. Generally delivery of the finished product is done within 10-12 days of order.

  • How accurate are the products with respect to the orders?

    The images shown and the product delivered are mostly similar however there might be slight alterations in shapes in the unforeseen event of rough transportation. Also photographic colours of wax and soap base can be of a slightly different shade than the real product.

  • Are these products safe for children?

    Care is taken to use only high-quality wax and soap base which do not cause any infection. However, care should be taken for small children as products are poisonous if intaken. Candles, when lit up, should be handled carefully. The soaps are completely safe for the children to use.

  • How safe are the colours and flavours used?

    Organic essence is used for the soaps and candles and crayon colours are used on wax. For soaps, the colours are imparted by natural substances used.

  • How long can I store the products?

    The finished goods are air sealed and can be stored for a long time before use.
