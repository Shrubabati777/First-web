FAQs about Chocolates

  • How long does it take to make these products?

    Depends on the number of orders at hand, the availability of the raw materials (the desired quantity and quality of chocolate and other ingredients) and the amount of minute details in the desired product. Generally delivery of the finished product is done within 10-12 days of order.

  • How accurate are the products with respect to the orders?

    The images shown and the product delivered are mostly similar however there might be slight alterations in shapes in the unforeseen event of rough transportation or excessive heat. Kindly refrigerate before consumption. Also, photographic colours of chocolate can be of a slightly different complexion than the real product.

  • Are these products safe for digestion?

    Care is taken to use only high-quality chocolate and other ingredients and the ordered product is hand-made with love.

  • What are the colours and flavours used?

    Food grade colours and natural flavouring substances are used.

  • How long can I store the products?

    The finished goods are air sealed and can be refrigerated for a considerable time before consumption.
